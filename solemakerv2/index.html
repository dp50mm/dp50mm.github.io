Step 1

Step off the sensor and calibrate.

Step 2

When the calibration is ready, step on the sensor.

Step 3

Gently shift your weight forward, back, left and right.

Step 4

Press stop to stop recording foot shape and weight.

Step 5

Personal adjustments can be made by selecting Change Contour or Change Cells.

Step 6

Wiggle your foot and check that the cell walls fall where your foot moves. Keep in mind that smaller cells provide more flexibility while larger cells more support.
